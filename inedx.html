Who We Are

Education is not a
privilege — it's a right.

Founded in 2019, BrightPath Foundation is a registered non-profit working across six Indian states to bridge the gap between quality education and marginalized communities.

We believe that a child's ZIP code should never determine the quality of their education. Through skill-building workshops, digital literacy programs, and scholarship initiatives, we are building a more equitable future — one student at a time.

What We Do

Programs Built for
Real Impact

Our four flagship programs address education at every stage — from foundational literacy to career readiness.

📚
Foundation
Padhega India Initiative

Remedial literacy and numeracy camps for out-of-school children aged 6–14. Operating in 40+ government schools across Rajasthan, MP and UP.

  • After-school learning centres
  • Bilingual learning materials
  • Monthly progress assessments
  • Parent community workshops
💻
Digital Skills
DigiDisha Program

Digital literacy bootcamps for adolescents from low-income families. From basic device usage to coding fundamentals and online safety.

  • Tablet & laptop training labs
  • Basic coding with Scratch & Python
  • Cyber safety curriculum
  • Career guidance sessions
👩‍🎓
Girls Education
Udaan – Girls Rise Program

A holistic empowerment program for girls aged 13–18 at risk of school dropout, combining education with menstrual health, life skills, and leadership.

  • Scholarship support
  • Menstrual health education
  • Leadership & public speaking
  • Safe space community groups
🛠️
Livelihood
SkillBridge Vocational

3–6 month vocational training courses for school dropouts aged 16–22 in high-demand trades: tailoring, electrical work, and digital services.

  • Industry-certified training
  • Soft skills & interview prep
  • Placement assistance
  • Micro-finance linkages
